# ChatGPT向け クリエイティブ生成プロンプト集（酒・ウイスキー×夜の美食）

> 使い方：**まず「0. ブランドブリーフ」を1回貼って**、その同じスレッドで 1〜5 の各プロンプトを順に投げる。出力は英語クリエイティブ。各プロンプトはコードブロックごとコピペでOK。
> 関連：商品/撮影リストは `sprint-kit-sake-whisky.md`、LP制作仕様は `data/outputs/sake-whisky-night-site-spec.md`。

---

## 0. ブランドブリーフ（最初にこれを貼る）

```
You are a senior creative director + short-form video scriptwriter for an inbound-Japan experience brand. Keep this brief in memory for everything that follows.

BRAND: "Tokyo Sake & Whisky Insider" — night sake & whisky experiences for foreign visitors, run by a native-English-speaking Japanese family.
EDGE (always lead with these 3): (1) native-English experts who genuinely know sake & whisky; (2) access to hidden / members-only bars and breweries you can't enter alone; (3) we help you buy & ship rare bottles home.
AUDIENCE: upper-middle Western/English-speaking travelers (30+), couples & food-and-drink lovers, repeat visitors who want the "real, inside" Japan. Confident, curious, hate tourist traps.
PRODUCTS:
- Product A (volume): "Tokyo Sake & Whisky Insider Night" — small group (4–8), 3–3.5h evening, 2–3 hidden bars, tasting flight + food pairing, native-English guide. Price ¥18,000–25,000/person.
- Product B (premium): "Private Japanese Whisky / Sake Pilgrimage" — fully private (1–4), members-only/brewery access, rare flight, buy & ship home. Price ¥90,000–150,000/group.
- Add-ons: private photographer; bespoke multi-day itinerary.
PLATFORMS: TikTok + Instagram Reels (primary), short vertical video. CTA always "Link in bio → booking page."
TONE: cinematic, premium-but-warm, insider, a little cheeky; never snobby. Always show price + access info on-screen for utility/saves.
HARD RULES (compliance): no health/efficacy claims about alcohol; nothing targeting minors or glorifying excess drinking; no "guaranteed entry / #1 in Japan" absolute claims; rare bottles are subject to availability — never promise stock.
WINNING FORMULAS TO MIRROR (proven inbound accounts): Paolo "A Day in the Life of ___" mini-doc; Japanese Whisky Dictionary "1 post = 1 bottle + specs on screen"; Magical Trip "bars you can't enter without a local" infiltration POV; John Gauntner "teach to build trust" education; Bayashi-style close-up ASMR of pours.
Reply "Ready" and wait for the next instruction.
```

## 1. 14本の短尺スクリプト＋キャプション（型A・SNS検証用）

```
Using the brand brief, write 14 short-form vertical videos for a 4-week launch (Weeks 1–4 below). For EACH video output a table row with:
- # and Title
- Week & objective (W1 authority/scarcity, W2 education/utility, W3 experience/social-proof, W4 conversion)
- Mirrors (which winning formula it copies)
- Hook (first 3 seconds, on-screen text + spoken)
- Shot-by-shot script (5–9 quick beats; note any on-screen price/access text)
- Spoken VO (≤45 words, natural English)
- Caption (1–2 lines) + 8–12 hashtags (mix broad + niche, e.g. #japantravel #tokyobar #japanesewhisky)
- On-screen CTA + "Link in bio" line
- Platform note (TikTok vs IG Reel emphasis)

Use these 14 themes (keep them, sharpen the wording):
W1: 1) "The Japanese whisky you literally can't buy abroad" 2) "A night in the life of a Tokyo whisky bar" 3) "Bars in Tokyo you can't enter without a local"
W2: 4) "How to order sake like a local (in English)" 5) "Junmai? Daiginjo? Sake explained in 60s" 6) "3 hidden Ginza whisky bars + how to actually get in"
W3: 7) "POV: your private whisky night in Tokyo" 8) "Guest reactions (UGC)" 9) "Why foreigners can't get into Japan's best bars — and how we do"
W4: 10) "Close-up ASMR tasting flight" 11) "Book the unbookable: members-only access" 12) "Guest testimonial + CTA" 13–14) two variants that re-use whichever concept is strongest, swapping bar/bottle.

Respect all HARD RULES. Keep VO punchy and skimmable. Output the full table.
```

## 2. 予約LPコピー（英語・本番文言）

```
Using the brand brief, write the final English copy for a one-page booking LP. Follow this section order and return ready-to-paste copy (headlines, subheads, body, button labels, microcopy):

1) HERO: H1 (max 7 words), 1-line subhead, primary button + secondary button.
2) THE TWO EXPERIENCES: a card each for Product A and Product B — name, 1-line promise, "includes" (4 bullets), duration, group size, price, button label.
3) WHY US (the 3 edges): 3 short blocks (heading + 1–2 sentences each).
4) WHAT YOU'LL TASTE: 1 short paragraph + 4–6 example bottles/styles (mark "examples, subject to availability").
5) TRUST: review-stars placeholder line + 3 short testimonial templates (so they can be swapped in).
6) FAQ: 6 Q&As (non-alcoholic option, allergies/halal, families/beginners, pre-payment / no cash at venue, cancellation policy, what if a bar is full).
7) FINAL CTA: 1 headline + button.
Also give 3 alternative H1 options.
Voice: cinematic, insider, warm. Obey all HARD RULES (no efficacy/absolute/under-age claims; availability caveats). Keep it skimmable.
```

## 3. OTA出品文（Viator / GetYourGuide / Klook）

```
Using the brand brief, write OTA listing copy for Product A ("Tokyo Sake & Whisky Insider Night"). Provide:
- Title (≤ ~65 chars, keyword-rich: include "Tokyo", "Sake", "Whisky", "Bar Hopping" or "Tasting", "Small Group")
- Short description (2–3 sentences)
- Full description (150–220 words)
- "What's included" / "Not included" bullet lists
- 6 highlights (bullet, benefit-led)
- "What to expect" itinerary (3 stops, ~bullet each)
- Important info (age 20+ / ID, dietary options, meeting point placeholder, cancellation)
- 10 search keywords/tags
Write it to rank and convert on Viator/GetYourGuide/Klook. Obey HARD RULES. Then give a 1-paragraph variant tuned for Klook's more Asia-leaning audience.
```

## 4. ビジュアル/撮影ディレクション＆AI画像プロンプト

```
Using the brand brief, produce a visual direction kit:
A) MOOD: 5 adjectives + a 2-line look-and-feel for the brand (lighting, color, framing for night bar footage).
B) SHOT LIST for the hero video (10 cinematic vertical shots: subject, framing, movement, ~duration).
C) 8 ready-to-use AI image prompts (for Midjourney/DALL·E style) for the LP and social — each describing a night Tokyo sake/whisky scene (no real logos, no identifiable faces, vertical 9:16 where relevant). Avoid copyrighted brand names on bottles.
D) On-screen text style guide (font feel, where price/access captions sit, subtitle style for accessibility).
Keep it production-ready for a small team shooting on a phone/mirrorless.
```

## 5. インフルエンサー打診DM＋メール（型A・コラボ用）

```
Using the brand brief, write outreach to inbound-Japan creators (food/travel/nightlife, English-speaking audiences) to invite them on Product A for free in exchange for content.
Provide:
- 3 short DM variants (Instagram/TikTok, ≤ 60 words, warm, specific, low-pressure)
- 1 longer email version (subject line + body) for bigger creators, including a simple deliverables ask (e.g., 1 Reel + 1 story set) and a soft affiliate offer (commission on bookings via their link)
- A 1-line follow-up nudge
Tone: peer-to-peer, generous, no desperation. Make it easy to say yes.
```

## 補足（運用メモ）
- 出力は必ず**HARD RULES（酒類の誇大/未成年/断定/在庫確約NG）**準拠か目視チェック。
- 当たった短尺（保存/再生が伸びた1〜2本）は、銘柄/バーを差し替えて**量産**（W4の13–14枠）。
- LP/OTA文言は、検証スプリントの実レビューが付き次第「信頼ブロック」に差し込み更新。
- ネイティブの最終トーン調整は家族で（ChatGPT出力は素材、最後の“本物感”は人で仕上げる）。
