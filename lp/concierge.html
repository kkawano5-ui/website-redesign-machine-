FAQ

Good to know

How do bookings and payments work?+

After a short brief we send a private proposal and a single, transparent quote. Travel and accommodation are contracted through our licensed travel partners; experiences and concierge services are arranged and hosted by us.

Are you licensed?+

We design and host experiences, and arrange regulated travel services in partnership with registered Japanese travel agents, so everything is handled correctly and accountably.

Who will host us?+

A native-English host who knows the venue, the craft and the etiquette — accompanied by accredited guides where required for a given site or region.

Which regions do you cover?+

Tokyo and Kyoto in depth, plus a curated set of regional "hero routes" — Kanazawa, the Setouchi islands, Hakuba & Niseko and others — where we can guarantee quality.

Cancellations & privacy?+

Terms are confirmed in writing with your proposal; reservation-only venues may carry their own deposit terms. Discretion is the default for every client.
