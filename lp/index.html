FAQ

Good to know

Do I have to drink alcohol?+

No — we offer non-alcoholic options and tea pairings. The experience is about the places and stories as much as the pour.

Allergies, halal or dietary needs?+

Tell us in advance and we'll plan around it wherever possible.

Is it good for beginners or couples?+

Absolutely. We tailor the depth to you — newcomers and seasoned drinkers both leave with something new.

Do I pay at each bar?+

Your tasting and food are pre-paid online — no fumbling with cash on the night.

What if a bar is full?+

We always plan backups. Access is curated for the night rather than tied to a single venue.

Cancellations?+

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before your night (details confirmed at booking).

Ready for a night you couldn't book on your own?

Tell us your dates and we'll hold your spots. Small groups fill fast.
